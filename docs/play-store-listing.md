## Full Description

Cycle is a minimalist period tracker for recording periods, reviewing recent history, and keeping a simple estimate of upcoming cycles.

Cycle is designed for people who want a quiet tracking tool without an account-first workflow. Your cycle data is stored locally by default. You can export and import your data when you need a backup or want to move it, and you can delete all local app data from the app settings.

Optional Google Calendar sync can add Cycle entries to a calendar you choose. Calendar sync is off unless you connect it, and you can disconnect it from settings.

Key features:

- Track period start and end dates.
- Review cycle history in a simple timeline.
- Adjust cycle and period length settings.
- Keep data on your device by default.
- Export and import your data.
- Optionally sync entries with Google Calendar.
- Delete all local Cycle data from settings.

Cycle is intended for personal tracking and organization. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, contraception, fertility prediction, or pregnancy prevention. Cycle estimates are informational only and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical guidance.
