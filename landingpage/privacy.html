Cycle Privacy Policy

Cycle is a minimalist period tracker. It stores period history locally on your device by default and does not run a developer-operated backend for your cycle history.

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This page is a practical privacy summary for the current app. It should be reviewed before production publication if hosting, domains, contact addresses, or Google OAuth configuration change.

What Cycle Stores

Cycle stores period dates, prediction settings, appearance settings, sync metadata, and import/export data locally on your device.

Google Calendar Sync

Google Calendar sync is optional. If you enable it, Cycle reads and writes period events in a Google Calendar you control so it can create, update, and remove Cycle events. Those events are stored by Google under your Google account settings.

What Cycle Does Not Use

  • No Cycle account.
  • No ads or analytics in the landing page or app.
  • No developer-run server storing your period history.
  • No sale of period history or app usage data.

Export And Import

Cycle can export a local backup file and import one back into the app. You control where that file is stored and shared.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to contact@cycleapp.org.
